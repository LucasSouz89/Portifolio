About me

I am Lucas Souza Silva, a technology student from Guarulhos, São Paulo. I am studying Game Development at FIAP, focusing on Unity, and I have a technical degree in Computer Networks from SENAI São Paulo. With certifications in cloud AI and data analysis, as well as proficiency in Microsoft Office 365 and Python, I have a solid technical foundation. My goal is to use my expertise to contribute to the field of technology.

EPIDEMIA

Epidemia is an exciting zombie apocalypse game where the goal is to survive as long as possible. Face hordes of zombies while shooting to defend yourself and collecting barrels that restore your health and provide ammunition. With intense graphics and action-packed gameplay, Epidemia challenges you to test your survival skills in a world devastated by the undead. Get ready to fight, survive, and surpass yourself in each round!

WIND CATCHERS

Wind Catcher is a hyper-casual game created by the Game Development course at FIAP, which highlights the importance of renewable energy. In the game, you control a wind turbine, collecting wind particles to increase energy production. Face challenges like balloons that act as obstacles and use batteries to repair damage. Wind Catcher educates about wind energy and promotes technological innovation for a sustainable future. The gameplay involves collecting wind and dodging obstacles to increase your score, reflecting the achieved energy efficiency. Join us on this journey to inspire new ideas and debates about sustainability!
